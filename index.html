Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our repair services

How long does a typical repair take?
Most screen and battery repairs are completed within 30-60 minutes. More complex issues like water damage may take 2-4 hours. We offer same-day service for most repairs.
Do you use genuine Apple parts?
We use genuine Apple parts whenever available. For older models where genuine parts are not available, we use high-quality OEM alternatives that meet or exceed Apple's standards.
Is there a warranty on repairs?
Yes, all our repairs come with a 30-day warranty that covers both parts and labor. This gives you peace of mind that your repair is protected.
Do I need an appointment?
While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure we can serve you promptly. You can book online or call us to schedule your repair.
Can you recover data from water-damaged devices?
In many cases, yes. We have specialized equipment to clean and dry water-damaged components. The sooner you bring in a water-damaged device, the higher the chance of data recovery.
